Social Media · UAE · 2023–2024

How Falconpack outscored a bigger competitor in 90 days

The Problem

Falconpack had a solid product but an underperforming social media presence. Their biggest competitor, Hotpack Global, had a larger audience and more posting frequency. Engagement per post was low and brand recall in the Gulf packaging market was weak.

What I Did

Rebuilt the content strategy from scratch across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and YouTube. Focused on a consistent brand voice, high-relevance product storytelling, and a posting rhythm calibrated for platform algorithms rather than just calendar slots. Ran competitive benchmarking against Hotpack Global weekly to measure share of engagement, not just follower count.

The Outcome

In three months, engagement climbed 69.01% and the page gained 49.1k followers. Falconpack was generating 5× more engagement per post than Hotpack Global — a brand with more followers and more posts per week. Customer retention and overall sales improved as a direct result of brand trust built through content.

"The benchmark isn't your follower count. It's how much of the conversation you own."

Google Ads · Real Estate · UAE · 2024

119 property leads. AED 498 total spend.

The Problem

ABC Properties was running Google Ads campaigns with decent traffic but poor conversion rates. Cost per lead was high and the quality of incoming enquiries was inconsistent — clicks that didn't turn into real buyer conversations.

What I Did

Audited the keyword structure and eliminated broad-match waste. Rewrote the ad copy to speak to high-intent buyers rather than general property browsers. Refined audience targeting parameters and adjusted bidding strategy to prioritize conversion value over traffic volume. Simultaneously managed their social media presence, which reinforced trust and brand recall for users who saw both paid and organic content.

The Outcome

The campaign produced 119 conversions on a total spend of AED 498 — approximately AED 4.18 per conversion. In UAE real estate, where cost per lead typically runs into hundreds of dirhams, this represented a significant efficiency gain. Organic social metrics also improved: 158.71% engagement rate, 924.9 average likes per post, placing ABC Properties at rank 101 in the UAE real estate sector.

"In UAE real estate, AED 4 per qualified lead isn't a campaign result. It's a competitive advantage."

Amazon FBA · E-Commerce · 2021–2023

Finding $100,000 that was already theirs

The Problem

Utopia Brands was operating Amazon FBA across the US, UK, Canada, Europe, and Turkey simultaneously. Inventory discrepancies, unreceived shipments, and unresolved reimbursement cases were quietly eroding margins. Most of these losses were technically recoverable — they just hadn't been pursued systematically.

What I Did

Led FBA inventory reconciliation across all five markets, working directly in Amazon Seller Central to audit shipment records, identify discrepancy patterns, and file cases for unclaimed reimbursements. Built a tracking system to monitor case status and follow up before deadlines. Simultaneously optimized product listings — rewriting titles, bullets, and A+ content — to improve conversion rates from existing traffic.

The Outcome

Recovered over $100,000 in unclaimed FBA shipments, resolving 95% of all open inventory cases. Amazon product listing optimizations resulted in a 15% increase in conversion rates. Also led a content team responsible for copy and concepts across the Utopia Brands product catalogue, including 1,500+ SKUs across multiple categories.

"Most Amazon sellers are sitting on recoverable money they don't know exists. The work is finding it."
